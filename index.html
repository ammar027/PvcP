Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Taskly. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Taskly does not collect any personal information. However, we may process the following data locally on your device:

  • Voice Data: Voice input for task recognition (processed and stored locally).
  • Usage Data: Task-related preferences and settings stored on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

All data is processed locally on your device to:

  • Enable voice-based task recognition.
  • Provide reminders and notifications.
  • Support task categorization and dark mode.

4. Data Security

We prioritize data security. All information remains on your device, and Taskly does not transmit or store any data externally.

5. Third-Party Services

Taskly does not share or transmit your data to third-party services.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data within the app. You can delete or modify your tasks, reminders, and preferences at any time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Please review it periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us.
